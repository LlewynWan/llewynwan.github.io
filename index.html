I'm Llewyn, Qian Wan (万骞/萬騫), an HCI researcher at City University of Hong Kong. I'm currently a PhD candidate in computer science supervised by Dr. Zhicong LU (陆志聪/陸志聰). I'm broadly interested in Human-centred AI, Creativity Support, AI-mediated communication, and Social Computing. I seek to understand the social impact of computing technologies, especially intelligent agents, and build interactive systems to facilitate social interactions, enhance creativity, and promote wellness.
